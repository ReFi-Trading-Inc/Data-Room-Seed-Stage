## üéØ What is ReFi.Trading?

ReFi.Trading is building the first transparent, non-custodial algorithmic trading platform powered by reinforcement learning (RL) agents. We enable retail traders and small fund managers to access institutional-grade trading automation without surrendering custody of their assets.

**The Opportunity:** 15 million active retail traders worldwide lack access to sophisticated quantitative strategies without giving up control of their funds. We're democratizing Wall Street AI while maintaining radical transparency.

**Key Differentiator:** We combine proven reinforcement learning technology (28% CAGR over 3+ years) with non-custodial architecture and cryptographic risk proofs‚Äîa unique position in the $18B market.
